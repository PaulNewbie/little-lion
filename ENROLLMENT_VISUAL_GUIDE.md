### Button Colors:
- **Gold/Yellow** (`#FFCB10`): Primary actions for staff (Add My Child)
- **Blue** (`#0052A1`): Secondary actions for admin (Guardian Account)

- **Blue**: Assessing/In Progress
- **Gold**: Enrolled/Featured
- **Gray**: Inactive/Disabled
